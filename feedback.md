## Tutor Comment
Excellent work Aisyah, Aaron and Winkle!! Your game was really nicely designed -- I loved that users could type in their names and the game will give a more personalised message when the game finishes. I also loved the addition of a timer -- it is a very natural extension, which makes your game more polished and interesting.

Making extra screens for the home and different game modes with instructions was also a really good addition - it made your UI more intuitive and easy for new users of your game.

You had helpful comments throughout your code, and you used Object oriented programming features well to separate chunks of logic. Your code was nicely formatted thoughout and the logic used to solve the tasks made your code very elegant, making your code a lot simpler and shorter than many other versions that I marked.

Your git commit messages were also quite descriptive and helpful. 

For the interesting challenges task, you had a very creative idea for selecting the different challenges -- having different shapes for the board made the game very interesting to play. There also had to be a way to determine what challenges are considered "easy" and "hard" and allow users to select a difficulty for the interesting challenges.

Brilliant work guys!! Your teamwork throughout the semester was really wonderful to see, and I had a lot of fun playing your implementation of the game!
